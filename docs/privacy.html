canopi

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

In short: canopi does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. The extension sends page text to our API for analysis and does not log or retain it.

What Canopi Does

canopi is a browser extension that checks environmental claims on product pages against EU and US consumer protection regulations. When you click "Scan this page," the extension extracts text content from the current page, sends it to our analysis API, and displays the results in the popup.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information — We do not collect your name, email, location, IP address, or any identifying information.
  • No browsing history — We do not track which websites you visit. The extension only activates when you manually click "Scan this page."
  • No cookies or tracking — We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics that identify individual users.
  • No accounts — canopi does not require registration or sign-in.

Data We Process

When you initiate a scan, the following data is sent to our API:

  • Page text content — The text extracted from the product page you are viewing.
  • Page URL — The URL of the page being scanned, used to identify the brand.

This data is processed in real-time to generate your scan results. It is not stored on our servers, not logged, not shared with third parties, and not used for advertising.

Data Stored Locally

The extension may store user preferences locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

canopi's analysis API is hosted on Railway.app. Railway processes the scan request and returns results. No data is retained after the request completes. We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Permissions Explained

  • activeTab — Required to read page content when you click "Scan." Only activates on explicit user action.
  • storage — Used to store local preferences on your device. No data is sent externally.

Children's Privacy

canopi does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data from any users, this is not applicable.

Your Rights

Under GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California):

  • Right to access — We hold no personal data, so there is nothing to access.
  • Right to deletion — We hold no personal data, so there is nothing to delete.
  • Right to object — We perform no profiling or automated decision-making about you.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance.
